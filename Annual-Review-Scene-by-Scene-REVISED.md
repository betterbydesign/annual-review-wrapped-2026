# Scott Foster — Year in Review “Unwrapped”
## Revised Scene-by-Scene Browser Story

*Window: July 2025 – June 2026*  
*Default audience: coworkers + leadership. Sensitive pricing, margin, and rate details are marked leadership-only and should not appear in a broad share version.*

---

## Global direction

- **Format:** browser-native story reel, not PowerPoint.
- **Pacing:** 12 scenes, 8–12 seconds each, ~2 minutes total if auto-played.
- **Copy density:** one headline, one metric, one caption per scene.
- **Navigation:** auto-advance, progress bar, pause, previous/next.
- **Visual style:** Altitude palette with Wrapped-style cards, bold contrast, layered geometry, and kinetic stat reveals.

- Full security incident names/details.
- Full recognition quotes.
- “Theme 1 / Theme 2” labels.
- Any unconfirmed metric stated as achieved.
